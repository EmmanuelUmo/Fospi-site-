Our Mission

To develop and implement data-driven, evidence-based sustainable intervention programs in the areas of education, gender equity, health and wellbeing, mental health, and poverty/hunger.

Through empirical research, problem analysis, and needs assessment, we aim to educate, empower, and positively impact individuals and communities. We strive to track, monitor, evaluate, and value the effectiveness and cost-efficiency of our interventions.

"Young people from different backgrounds have a huge impact on us and all our activities. They highlight those parts of the society that are broken, so we can help them in all possible ways to regain hope and flourish in life."

— FOSPI Foundation Team

Our Vision & Values

Guided by evidence-based principles and unwavering commitment to transparency and impact.

🌍 Our Vision

A world where every individual has equal access to education, opportunities, and resources, resulting in sustainable development in education outcomes, gender equity, mental health awareness, health and wellbeing, and poverty alleviation.

⚖️ Our Values

Evidence-based interventions, transparency in all operations, community-centered approach, sustainable impact, and unwavering commitment to empowering marginalized communities across Nigeria.

🎯 Our Impact

We've improved the lives of over 1,000 young people through our evidence-based initiatives, providing education, healthcare awareness, and essential skills to help them thrive and regain hope.

Our Strategic Approach

A systematic, evidence-based methodology that ensures maximum impact and sustainable outcomes.

1

Develop

We develop data-driven and evidence-based sustainable intervention programs through comprehensive research and analysis of community needs.

2

Implement

We implement these programs to target and stimulate positive outcomes and impacts in underserved communities across Nigeria.

3

Educate

We aim to educate individuals and communities through our interventions, building capacity and knowledge for long-term sustainability.

4

Empower

Our interventions are designed to empower marginalized groups and promote gender equity, creating lasting change from within communities.

5

Evaluate

We track, monitor, and evaluate the effectiveness and cost-efficiency of our interventions to ensure continuous improvement and accountability.
